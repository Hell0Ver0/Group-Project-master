    Sam Hampton

    Co-founder Live Dance


    Hi, I'm Sam. I'm an arts marketing professional who has worked for the British Council's Theatre and Dance team, East London Dance and London's international performing arts festival LIFT. I help to deliver theatre and dance projects in the UK and across the world, whilst also writing blogs, newsletters with the latest in the performing arts world. Classically trained in ballet, contemporary and jazz I still occasionally choreograph for student groups and try to catch a dance class or a show when I can!
